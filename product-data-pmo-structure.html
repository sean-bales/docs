Product expands to include Data. PMO splits into its own function.

Effective 06/01/2026. Broader announcement 05/22/2026.

01Structural shift

Data becomes a product. Product leads initiatives. PMO frees product to focus.

01
Data becomes a product, not an analytics service.
The team enriches core data so it is accurate, real-time, and useful enough to drive strategy and revenue. Editorial, product, sales, and finance get governed access in the formats they actually use, not dashboards to interpret.
02
Product officially leads initiatives.
Subscriptions get the primary emphasis. Senior leadership team decides weekly. Ends the stakeholder and product loops that stalled at consensus and replaces them with a single forum where decisions get made.
03
PMO stands up as its own dedicated function.
Dedicated project management keeps work organized, communication tight, and alignment current. Frees the product team to focus on product work.

Sr. Director leads Data and Product. PMO operates as a peer function.
