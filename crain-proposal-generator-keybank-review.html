Risks and assumptions.

Seven items the agent flagged for AE review before send. Verbatim from the output.

  1. C-Suite Conversations date TBD. If it lands before May 7, the 14-day booking lead is tight. Confirm with events team.
  2. Executive Insights Roundtable 84-day lead. Earliest print insert publishes mid-July. Scheduled accordingly.
  3. Politics exclusion vs. CCB editorial. CCB covers Chicago politics as core editorial. Resolve via content-category targeting exclusions at ad-server level. Confirm with ad ops.
  4. Publicis rates. Fallon is Publicis Worldwide. If Crain holds a Publicis MSA, final invoicing may shift. Confirm with finance.
  5. 75% MRC viewability. CCB display typically exceeds 75%. MOAT tags accepted. State explicitly in deck.
  6. Budget framing. $300K as ceiling. Fallon’s email asked for a recommendation. Mix scales if agency goes higher.
  7. Frequency caps not published. No per-product caps on CCB’s sheet. Standard caps configured at ad-server level to Fallon’s preference.
